            Summary: This gene is a member of the family of serotonin
            receptors, which are G protein coupled receptors that stimulate
            cAMP production in response to serotonin (5-hydroxytryptamine). The
            gene product is a glycosylated transmembrane protein that functions
            in both the peripheral and central nervous system to modulate the
            release of various neurotransmitters. Multiple transcript variants
            encoding proteins with distinct C-terminal sequences have been
            described. [provided by RefSeq, May 2010].
