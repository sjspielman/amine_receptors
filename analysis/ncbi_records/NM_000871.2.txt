            Summary: This gene encodes a protein that belongs to the
            seven-transmembrane G protein-coupled receptor family of proteins.
            The encoded protein couples with the Gs alpha subunit and
            stimulates adenylate cyclase to activate the cyclic AMP-dependent
            signaling pathway. This receptor is thought to regulate cholinergic
            neuronal transmission in the brain. Several antidepressants and
            antipsychotic drugs have a high affinity for this receptor.
