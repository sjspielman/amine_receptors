            Summary: The neurotransmitter serotonin (5-hydroxytryptamine, 5-HT)
            has been implicated in a wide range of psychiatric conditions and

            described in this record is a member of 5-hydroxytryptamine
            (serotonin) receptor family and encodes a multi-pass membrane
            protein that functions as a receptor for 5-hydroxytryptamine and
            couples to G-proteins. This protein has been shown to function in
            part through the regulation of intracellular Ca2+ mobilization.
